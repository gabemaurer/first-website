## üé¨ Content Creation Tips

### Video Length
- **Sweet spot**: 15-30 seconds for maximum engagement
- **Hook time**: First 3 seconds are critical - start strong!
- **Max length**: Keep under 60 seconds for TikTok

### Pacing
- **3-4 seconds per clip** is ideal
- Fast cuts maintain attention
- Slow motion only for KEY moments (1-2 per video)
- Speed up boring transitions

### Transitions
- Use fade for smooth, calm transitions
- Use zoom for energy and excitement
- Mix them up - don't use same transition twice in a row
- AI will suggest best placement

## üé® Visual Style Tips

### Filters
- **Vibrant**: Great for outdoor, colorful content
- **Vintage**: Perfect for nostalgic vibes
- **B&W**: Use sparingly for dramatic effect
- Don't overuse - 1-2 filters max per video

### Composition
- Keep subjects centered for mobile viewing
- Remember: viewers watch in portrait mode
- Leave space at top/bottom for captions
- Important elements in middle third of frame

## üéµ Audio Tips

### Volume Control
- Lower volume during text/caption moments
- Full volume for action scenes
- Fade out at end for smooth finish
- AI will suggest optimal levels

### Music Sync (when adding music)
- Sync beat drops with clip changes
- Match energy level to audio
- Use speed effects on beat
